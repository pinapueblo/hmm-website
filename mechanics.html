Kinematics

  • Velocity: \[\ \vec{v} =\frac{\Delta s}{\Delta t} = \frac{ds}{dt}\]
  • Acceleration: \[\ \vec{a} =\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{dv}{dt}\]
  • Velocity: \[v = v_0 + at\]
  • Displacement: \[x = v_0 t + \frac{1}{2} a t^2\]
  • Final Velocity: \[v^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x-x_0)\]
  • Average Velocity: \[v_{avg} = \frac{v_0 + v}{2}\]
  • Free Fall: \[y = y_0 + v_0 t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2\]
  • Projectile Motion: \[x = v_0 \cos(\theta) t, y = v_0 \sin(\theta) t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2\]
  • Range of Projectile: \[R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}\]

Dynamics

  • Newton's Second Law: \[\sum F = ma = \frac{dp}{dt}\]
  • Weight: \[W = mg\]
  • Dry Friction: \[f \leq \mu_sN\] \[f = \mu_kN\]
  • Centripetal Force: \[F_c = \frac{mv^2}{r}\]
  • Momentum: \[p = mv\]
  • Conservation of Momentum: \[p_{i} = p_{f}\]
  • Impulse: \[J = \Delta p = F \Delta t = \int F \,dt\]
  • Elastic Collision: \[m_1v_{1i} + m_2v_{2i} = m_1v_{1f} + m_2v_{2f}\]
  • Inelastic Collision: \[m_1v_{1i} + m_2v_{2i} = (m_1 + m_2)v_f\]

Rotational Motion

  • Rotational Motion: \[\tau = I \alpha\]
  • Moment of Inertia: \[I = \sum m_i r_i^2\]
  • Angular Momentum: \[L = I \omega\]
  • Conservation of Angular Momentum: \[L_i = L_f\]
  • Torque: \[\tau = rF \sin(\theta)\]
  • Angular Velocity: \[\omega = \frac{\Delta \theta}{\Delta t} = \frac{d\theta}{dt}\]
  • Angular Acceleration: \[\alpha = \frac{\Delta \omega}{\Delta t} = \frac{d\omega}{dt}\]
  • Rolling Motion: \[v = r\omega\]
  • Centripetal Acceleration: \[a_c = \frac{v^2}{r} = r\omega^2\]
  • Angular Kinetic Energy: \[KE_{rot} = \frac{1}{2} I \omega^2\]
  • Angular Velocity: \[\omega = \frac{2\pi}{T}\]
  • Angular Displacement: \[\theta = \omega t + \frac{1}{2} \alpha t^2\]
  • Final Angular Velocity: \[\omega_f = \omega_i + \alpha t\]
  • Average Angular Velocity: \[\omega_{avg} = \frac{\omega + \omega_0}{2}\]
  • Angular Momentum: \[L = I \omega\]
  • Angular Frequency: \[\omega = 2\pi f\]
  • Moment of Inertia for Common Shapes:
    • Solid Cylinder: \[I = \frac{1}{2} m r^2\]
    • Solid Sphere: \[I = \frac{2}{5} m r^2\]
    • Thin Rod (about center): \[I = \frac{1}{12} m L^2\]

Energy

  • Work: \[W = F \Delta d \cos(\theta)\]
  • Kinetic Energy: \[KE = \frac{1}{2} mv^2\]
  • Gravitational Potential Energy: \[PE = mg \Delta h\]
  • Conservation of Energy: \[KE_i + PE_i = KE_f + PE_f\]
  • Power: \[P = \frac{\Delta W}{\Delta t} =\frac{dW}{dt}\]
  • Mechanical Energy: \[E_{mech} = KE + PE\]
  • Work-Energy Theorem: \[W = \Delta KE = KE_f - KE_i\]
  • Work Done by a Variable Force: \[W = \int F \, dx\]
  • Elastic Potential Energy: \[PE_{elastic} = \frac{1}{2} k x^2\]

Gravitational Motion

  • Gravitational Force: \[F_g = \frac{G m_1 m_2}{r^2}\]
  • Gravitational Potential Energy: \[PE_g = -\frac{G m_1 m_2}{r}\]
  • Escape Velocity: \[v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{r}}\]
  • Kepler's Third Law: \[T^2 = \frac{4\pi^2}{GM} r^3\]
  • Orbital Velocity: \[v_o = \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}\]

Statics and Solids

  • Distributed Load: \[q(x) = -\frac{d^2M}{dx^2}\]
  • Shear Force: \[V(x) = \frac{dM}{dx}\]
  • Distributed Load: \[F = \int_{0}^{L}q(x) \,dx \]
  • Moment of Distributed Load: \[M = \int_{0}^{L} xq(x) \, dx\]
  • Moment Arm: \[\bar{x} = \frac{int_{0}^{L} xq(x) \, dx}{\int_{0}^{L}q(x) \,dx} \]
  • Parallel Axis Theorem: \[I = \sum (I_{cm} + d^2A)\]
  • Location of Centroid:
  • \[x_c = \frac{\sum x_i A_i}{\sum A_i}\]
  • \[y_c = \frac{\sum y_i A_i}{\sum A_i}\]
  • Area Moment of Inertia:
  • \[I_x = \int y^2 \,dA\]
  • \[I_y = \int x^2 \,dA\]
  • Polar Moment of Inertia: \[J = \int r^2 \,dA\]
  • Normal Stress: \[\sigma = \frac{F}{A}\]
  • Shear Stress: \[\tau = \frac{V}{A}\]
  • Normal Strain: \[\epsilon = \frac{\Delta L}{L_0} = \frac{dL}{L_0}\]
  • Shear Strain: \[\gamma = \frac{\Delta x}{L} = \frac{d\theta}{dx}\]
  • Hooke's Law: \[\sigma = E \epsilon\]
  • Shear Modulus: \[\tau = G \gamma\]
  • Poisson's Ratio: \[\nu = -\frac{\epsilon_y}{\epsilon_x}\]
  • Torsional Shear Stress: \[\tau = \frac{Tr}{J}\]
  • Angle of Twist: \[\theta = \frac{TL}{GJ}\]
  • Bending Stress: \[\sigma = \frac{My}{I}\]
  • Bending Deformation (Deflection): \[\frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = \frac{M}{EI}\]
  • Shear Stress in Beams: \[\tau = \frac{VQ}{Ib}\]
