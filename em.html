Electromagnetism

  • Coulomb's Law: \[F = k_e \frac{|q_1 q_2|}{r^2}\]
  • Electric Field: \[E = \frac{F}{q}\]
  • Gauss's Law: \[\Phi_E = \oint \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{A} = \frac{Q_{enc}}{\varepsilon_0}\]
  • Electric Potential: \[V = k_e \frac{q}{r}\]
  • Capacitance: \[C = \frac{Q}{V} = \frac{\kappa \varepsilon_0 A}{d}\]
  • Capacitors:
    • Series: \[\frac{1}{C_{s}} = \sum_{i} \frac{1}{C_i}\]
    • Parallel: \[C_{p} = \sum_{i} C_i\]
  • Energy Stored in a Capacitor: \[U = \frac{1}{2} CV^2 = \frac{1}{2} QV = \frac{Q^2}{2C}\]
  • Current: \[I = \frac{dQ}{dt}\]
  • Ohm's Law: \[V = IR\]
  • Kirchhoff's Laws:
    • \[\sum I_{in} = \sum I_{out}\]
    • \[\sum V = 0\] (in a closed loop)
  • Resistance: \[R = \rho \frac{L}{A}\]
  • Resistors:
    • Series: \[R_{s} = \sum_{i} R_i \]
    • Parallel: \[\frac{1}{R_{p}} = \sum_{i} \frac{1}{R_i}\]
  • Power: \[P = IV = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R}\]
  • Magnetic Force: \[F = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B})\]
  • Magnetic Force on a Current-Carrying Wire: \[\vec{F} = I \vec{L} \times \vec{B}\]
  • Lorentz Force: \[\vec{F} = q(\vec{E} + \vec{v} \times \vec{B})\]
  • Magnetic Field: \[B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}\]
  • Faraday's Law of Induction: \[\mathcal{E} = -\frac{d\Phi_B}{dt}\]
  • Ampère's Law: \[\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0 I_{enc}\]

Electromagnetic Waves

  • Wave Equation: \[\frac{\partial^2 E}{\partial t^2} = c^2 \nabla^2 E\]
  • Speed of Light: \[c = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0 \varepsilon_0}}\]
  • Poynting Vector: \[\vec{S} = \vec{E} \times \vec{B}\]
  • Maxwell's Equations:
    • Gauss's Law for Electricity: \[\nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}\]
    • Gauss's Law for Magnetism: \[\nabla \cdot \vec{B} = 0\]
    • Faraday's Law: \[\nabla \times \vec{E} = -\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t}\]
    • Ampère-Maxwell Law: \[\nabla \times \vec{B} = \mu_0 (\vec{J} + \varepsilon_0 \frac{\partial \vec{E}}{\partial t})\]
    • Energy Desnity: \[\frac{1}{2} \varepsilon_0 E^2 + \frac{1}{2 \mu_0} B^2 \]

Electrostatics

  • Electric Flux: \[\Phi_E = \int \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{A}\]
  • Electric Potential: \[\vec{E} = -\Delta V \]
  • Potential Energy: \[U = k_e \frac{q_1 q_2}{r}\]
  • Electric Dipole Moment: \[\vec{p} = q \vec{d}\]
  • Torque on a Dipole: \[\vec{\tau} = \vec{p} \times \vec{E}\]

Magnetostatics

  • Magnetic Flux: \[\Phi_B = \int \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{A}\]
  • Biot-Savart Law: \[d\vec{B} = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{I d\vec{l} \times \hat{r}}{r^2}\]
  • Ampère's Circuital Law: \[\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0 I_{enc}\]
