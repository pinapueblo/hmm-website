Fluids

  • Continuity Equation: \[\rho_1 A_1 v_1 = \rho_2 A_2 v_2\]
  • Bernoulli's Equation: \[P + \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 + \rho gh = \text{constant}\]
  • Hydrostatic Pressure: \[P = P_0 + \rho gh\]
  • Pascal's Principle: \[F_1/A_1 = F_2/A_2\]
  • Archimedes' Principle: \[F_b = \rho_f V_d g\]
  • Reynolds Number: \[Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu}\]
  • Surface Tension: \[\gamma = F/L\]
  • Capillarity: \[h = \frac{2\gamma \cos(\theta)}{\rho g r}\]

Fluid Dynamics

  • Navier-Stokes Equation: \[\rho \left( \frac{\partial \vec{v}}{\partial t} + (\vec{v} \cdot \nabla) \vec{v} \right) = -\nabla P + \mu \nabla^2 \vec{v}\]
  • Flow Rate: \[Q = A v\]
  • Viscosity: \[\tau = \mu \frac{du}{dy}\]
  • Poiseuille's Law: \[Q = \frac{\pi r^4 (P_1 - P_2)}{8 \mu L}\]

Applications

  • Venturi Effect: \[P_1 + \frac{1}{2} \rho v_1^2 = P_2 + \frac{1}{2} \rho v_2^2\]
  • Torricelli's Law: \[v = \sqrt{2gh}\]
  • Flow in Pipes: \[h_f = \frac{f L}{D} \cdot \frac{\rho v^2}{2g}\]
