Thermodynamics

  • First Law of Thermodynamics: \[\Delta U = Q - W\]
  • Ideal Gas Law: \[PV = nRT\]
  • Work Done by Gas: \[W = \int P \, dV\]
  • Heat Transfer: \[Q = mc\Delta T\]
  • Efficiency: \[\eta = \frac{W_{out}}{Q_{in}}\]
  • Carnot Efficiency: \[\eta_{carnot} = 1 - \frac{T_c}{T_h}\]
  • Entropy Change: \[\Delta S = \frac{Q}{T}\]
  • Specific Heat Capacity: \[c = \frac{Q}{m\Delta T}\]

Thermodynamic Processes

  • Isothermal Process: \[PV = nRT\]
  • Adiabatic Process: \[PV^\gamma = \text{constant}\]
  • Isochoric Process: \[\Delta U = Q\]
  • Isobaric Process: \[Q = \Delta U + P\Delta V\]

Heat Transfer

  • Conduction: \[Q = kA\frac{\Delta T}{d}\]
  • Convection: \[Q = hA\Delta T\]
  • Radiation: \[Q = \sigma A T^4\]
  • Stefan-Boltzmann Law: \[P = \sigma A (T^4 - T_{env}^4)\]
  • Newton's Law of Cooling: \[\frac{dT}{dt} = -k(T - T_{env})\]

Phase Changes

  • Latent Heat: \[Q = mL\]
  • Heat of Fusion: \[Q_f = mL_f\]
  • Heat of Vaporization: \[Q_v = mL_v\]
  • Clausius-Clapeyron Equation: \[\frac{dP}{dT} = \frac{L}{T(V_g - V_l)}\]
